Online Education

Internet makes people life easier than before

Online education means learn in online accordance with internet from distance

There is no time barriers in online education (use class)

Internet and device are the main element of online education (use id)

Once upon a time, when people say about education, our mind created an image like "benches, classroom, teacher, blackboard, duster etc". But now image has totally changed. Students and teacher both connected with internet, study topics come to a computer/ laptop/ mobile screen.
Online education makes to get education from abroad. Teachers solve their problems with live chatting or personal group discussion. Assignment or other hometask has done by Microsoft Excel, Word, Powerpoint etc.

People become more upgrade with online education system. Not only academic study but also personal development skill related topic like: freelancing, various language, software skill improvement, innovation can be learned among it.
Online education saves time to go institution, saves transport expense and it is faster than classroom education because it has no time barriers. But on the other side, student can be inattentive in lesson or do any other work in class time. Everything has a dual effect so online education also has bad sides.
Online education gives people wider path to go and improve personal skills. Specially for women who got married and have children. Women also can do any job like teaching, designing, writing, painting, marketing and so many through online.
Online education make people life more flexible. Who are concern about their goal, it's a vast opportunity for dream achiever.
